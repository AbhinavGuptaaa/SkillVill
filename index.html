About Us

Welcome to SkillVill – your gateway to authentic rural experiences!

In a world that’s rapidly modernizing, we often lose touch with the roots of traditional skills, crafts, and agriculture. SkillVill bridges this gap by connecting urban explorers and Gen Z with skilled farmers, artisans, and rural communities. Our platform offers immersive, hands-on experiences where users can engage in activities like farming, pottery, fishing, weaving, dairy farming, and much more.


By booking a personalized skill-based tour, you don’t just learn—you contribute. Every experience directly supports rural artisans, helping preserve traditional skills while boosting local economies.


At SkillVill, we believe in sustainable tourism, digital empowerment, and cultural revival. Whether you’re seeking a break from city life, a unique adventure, or a deeper understanding of rural craftsmanship, we’ve got the perfect experience for you!


Join us in celebrating heritage, empowering artisans, and creating unforgettable memories—one village at a time.
